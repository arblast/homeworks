Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (skin & bone in preferred)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Cooking wine
  • Chicken broth
  • Butter
  • Herbs (optional)

Braised Chicken

Description

This is a recipe on the basic preparation of chicken. required ingredients are easily found in every kitchen. Preparation time is minimal, and it is very easy.
The preparation method is commonly used in many dishes, and has two main steps: browning and braising.

Directions

  1. Pat dry the chicken thighs using paper towels.
  2. Rub salt and pepper on both sides of the chicken.
  3. Heat up a pan to high heat, then put some olive oil in.
  4. Once pan is hot, place each thigh in skin side down.
  5. Once skin is brown(about 1 min), flip the thighs and cook for another minute.
  6. Once the thighs are browned on both sides pour in some cooking wine and scrape pan with spatula.
  7. After alcohol is cooked off (about 30s), pour in the chicken stock and turn to low heat.
  8. Place butter and herbs into the stock to flavor the sauce.
  9. Simmer for about 10 minutes, depending on the size and amount of chicken thighs.
  10. Remove chicken into plate and pour sauce over.
